About

Parking is a major hassle here at the University of Texas at Austin. For our Advanced Programming and App Development course project, we decided to design and develop a solution that lets a user see a list of garages around campus and the number of spots available at each garage. They may choose to reserve a spot for a limited amount of time. At the garage entrance, the user is to verify that they had a reservation and occupy that reservation. The solution also keeps track of how long a user has parked at the spot and can provide support for third-party pluggable billing services.

Made by Abhi and Vrinda.
